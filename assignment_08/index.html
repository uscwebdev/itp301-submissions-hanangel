Final Project Proposal

TOPIC

My website will be about the Musee de l'Orangerie, an art museum in Paris that houses many of Monet's works.

It will include a landing page for the museum, brief history and background of the museum, a gallery of the art pieces there, and an online museum gift shop where you can browse the products sold by the museum.

AUDIENCE

My audience will include tourists who are looking for activities to do in Paris, as well as individuals who are interested in learning more about art and want to appreciate artworks in person. Additionally, individuals who are admirers of Monet's artworks and art style would find this website useful to them.

DESIGN & STYLE

My website will be clean and modern with a more neutral color palette so that the focus remains on the art. The images of the art will be larger in size, compared to the rest of the content on the page. Main logos will use a serif font, but smaller text will be in a sans-serif font to increase readability.

COLOR PALETTE

The color palette will include black, white, neutrals, and pastel accents to reflect Monet's works.

INSPIRATION

  • Ohio University's School of Art + Design
  • White Art Walk Studio
  • In Praise of Painting: Dutch Masterpieces at The Met
  • Georgia O'Keeffe at the MoMA

SCOPE

The website will include four pages.

The first page will be a landing page for the museum that includes basic information about upcoming exhibits and new events at the museum. The second page will be a brief content page about the history of the museum and the main featured artist, Monet. The third page will be a gallery of the works of Monet and other artists showcased at the museum. The fourth page will be a museum gift shop where you can browse items that are sold by the museum.
